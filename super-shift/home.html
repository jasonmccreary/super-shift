HERE'S HOW IT ALL WORKS

1

Connect Git Account

Authenticate Shift using your GitHub, Bitbucket, or GitLab account and give the Shift robot crew temporary access to your repos. Don’t worry, it’s safe.

2

Choose Project Repo

Pick your desired shift, from 4.2 to 5.3 and everything in between, set your Git repo and branch, and insert some coins with our simple checkout process.

3

Let the Magic Happen

Everything is automated from here. Changes are peformed on a branch and a highly detailed Pull Request will be ready for your review in a few moments.

MUST BE EXPENSIVE, RIGHT?

Nope. We want everyone to be able to take advantage of Shift, so we’re keeping the cost as low as possible.
